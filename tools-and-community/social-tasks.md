# Social Tasks

Social Tasks allow community members to complete simple activities and earn Qantera contributor points.

### Example tasks

Tasks may include:

* follow Qantera on X;
* like an official post;
* repost an official post;
* join the Telegram channel;
* join the Telegram community group.

### How it works

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Connect the required social account.
3. Complete the task.
4. Return to the Qantera application.
5. Select **Verify**.
6. Receive points after successful verification.

### Important rules

* Each task can normally be completed once per eligible account.
* Duplicate or automated accounts may be rejected.
* Removed social actions may cause points to be removed.
* Task values may change between campaigns.
* The live task page is the source of truth for rewards and eligibility.

### Wallet multiplier

Some campaigns may provide additional points for users connecting through a supported wallet such as OKX Wallet.

The multiplier and eligibility rules will be displayed on the active campaign page.
