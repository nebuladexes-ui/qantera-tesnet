# Contributor leaderboard

The Contributor Leaderboard tracks eligible activity across the Qantera ecosystem.

### Activities that may earn points

Points may come from:

* Social Tasks;
* Yap Tasks;
* testnet transactions;
* smart-contract deployment;
* Genesis NFT participation;
* community contributions;
* approved bug reports.

### Leaderboard information

The leaderboard may display:

* wallet address or username;
* total points;
* rank;
* completed tasks;
* badges;
* contributor streak;
* Genesis NFT status.

### Point verification

Scores may be reviewed to remove:

* duplicate accounts;
* automated activity;
* fake engagement;
* copied content;
* manipulated transactions;
* other Sybil behavior.

### Important

Contributor points are campaign scores.

Unless officially announced, points do not represent:

* QTER tokens;
* ownership;
* guaranteed rewards;
* a financial claim.

The live leaderboard and campaign rules are the source of truth for scoring and eligibility.
