# Qantera Origin NFT

The Qantera Origin NFT is designed as an on-chain credential for early contributors to the Qantera ecosystem.

### What it represents

The NFT may represent:

* early testnet participation;
* completed community activities;
* contributor identity;
* participation in the Qantera Genesis phase.

### Possible eligibility requirements

Eligibility may include:

* connecting a wallet;
* completing Social Tasks;
* completing a Yap Task;
* performing testnet transactions;
* reaching a minimum point level.

The final requirements will be displayed on the official mint page.

### Mint information

The following details will be added before minting begins:

* NFT contract address;
* total supply;
* mint start;
* mint end;
* mint price;
* maximum per wallet;
* required points;
* transfer rules;
* NFT utility.

### Important

The Origin NFT does not automatically guarantee:

* token rewards;
* airdrops;
* financial value;
* mainnet privileges.

Only use the official Qantera mint page and verify chain ID `974621` before signing.
