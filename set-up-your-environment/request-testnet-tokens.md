# Request testnet tokens

When the faucet opens:

1. Open the official Qantera faucet.
2. Connect your testnet wallet.
3. Switch to Qantera Public Testnet.
4. Confirm chain ID `974621`.
5. Check the receiving address.
6. Select **Claim 0.1 QTER**.
7. Wait for the transaction to complete.
8. View the transaction on the [Qantera Explorer](https://explorer.qantera.network/).

### What QTER is used for

You can use testnet QTER to:

* send transactions;
* pay gas fees;
* deploy smart contracts;
* interact with applications;
* complete eligible on-chain testnet activities.

### Faucet safety

The official faucet will never request:

* your seed phrase;
* your private key;
* payment from another network;
* remote access to your device;
* an unlimited token approval.
